§n§6Game Enhancements§r
* Added ReAuth to prevent having to reload pack if login disconnects
* Changed Nether generation to BetterNether, for more even distrobution of ores.
* Removed Actually Additions and some Rustic plants from world generation. Added them to Farming for Blockheads market, gated behind appropriate currency, along with tooltips.
* Removed Matter Overdrive gravitational anomalies from Overworld generation.
* Removed Crystal Clusters from world generation
* Unstaged Actually Additions Solidified Experience, Balls of Fur, and Tiny Coal/Charcoal
* Removed Mystical World silkworms from generation
* Reduced spawn weight of Foxhounds
* Staged Thermal Foundation mobs
* Disabled Charm Spectres and Nether Gold Deposits
* Unstaged Random Things Lava Charm
* Added Charged Emerald recipe - 1.21 gigawatts!
* Added custom textures by Mar3xgg for Electroblob's Wizardry, Matter Overdrive, and Metallurgy
* Added custom Ender ore that drops dust that can be smelted into Ender Pearls.
* Unstaged Thermal Expansion basic energy cell for early game power storage

§n§6Bug Fixes§r
* Fixed staging issue with Congealed Slime

* Removed Soul Sand Chickens from natural spawns
* Staged Chickens-related JEI screens
* Added recipes for Bibliocraft Printing Press, Typesetting Table, and Print Press Chase
* Staged Holding enchat into Thermal stage
